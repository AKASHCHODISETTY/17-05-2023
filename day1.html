THE BIRTH:

Kalyan was born on 2 September in 1968 or 1971 to Konidela Venkata Rao and Anjana Devi in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh.He is the younger brother of Chiranjeevi and Nagendra Babu.

JOURNEY TOWARDS INDUSTRY:

Pawan Kalyan, whose real name is Konidela Kalyan Babu, is a popular actor and politician in the Telugu film industry. He was born on September 2, 1971, in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh, India. Pawan Kalyan is the younger brother of renowned actor Chiranjeevi and actor-producer Nagendra Babu. Pawan Kalyan made his acting debut in 1996 with the Telugu film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi." However, he gained significant recognition and success with his second film, "Tholi Prema" (1998), which became a blockbuster hit and won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu. This film marked a turning point in his career and established him as a leading actor in the industry. Over the years, Pawan Kalyan has acted in several successful films, including "Gabbar Singh," "Attarintiki Daredi," "Jalsa," and "Gopala Gopala," among others. His unique style, charismatic screen presence, and powerful dialogue delivery have earned him a massive fan following in the Telugu-speaking states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana

KALION INTO POLITICS:

Apart from his acting career, Pawan Kalyan is also known for his involvement in politics. In 2008, he founded the political party called Praja Rajyam Party (PRP) with the aim of bringing about social change and serving the people. However, the party did not perform well in the 2009 elections, and Pawan Kalyan subsequently withdrew from active politics.In 2014, Pawan Kalyan formed the Jana Sena Party (JSP) with a mission to fight for the rights of the common man and work towards the development of Andhra Pradesh. He actively campaigned for the 2014 general elections in support of the Telugu Desam Party (TDP) and Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) alliance. In 2019, he contested the elections himself and won the Bhimavaram assembly seat but lost in the Gajuwaka constituency.
